About Merryland International School

Merryland International School with strength of 3,000 pupils and 400 staff is a testament of success; concrete proof of the fact that Mrs. George had enough courage to wear the wings of hope and fly into the skies of achievement. Yes, we have come a long way from the dream that was conceived in the remote hills of Southern India. Surely, no accolade could befit the lady – and the dream – more than the wish to see these storehouses of knowledge and life continue nurturing the generations to come.

Curriculum

The school is accredited by the University of Cambridge, and offers Cambridge Primary, Cambridge IGCSE and Cambridge AS & A level Courses. This International curriculum develops students’ skills in creative thinking, enquiry and problem solving, and gives them excellent preparation for the next stage in their education.

Curriculum in Merryland International is divided into five stages:
  • Kindergarden
  • Cambridge Primary
  • Cambridge Secondary
  • Cambridge IGCSE
  • Cambridge Advanced

Merryland International School complies with the Cambridge progression test offered at the end of all stages. An external bench mark examination from Cambridge, ‘Check point examination’ test the learners at the end of year 6 and year 8 in literacy, numeracy and scientific knowledge to be in track with IGCSE.
